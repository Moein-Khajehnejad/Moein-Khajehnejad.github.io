Currently I am a visiting scholar at Max Planck Institute for Software Systems, working under the supervision of Professor Adish Singla the head of Machine Teaching group .

My research aims to make sense of large amounts of data by developing and applying algorithms and techniques in areas including

  • social and information network analysis,
  • machine learning,
  • computational social science,
  • data mining,
  • natural language processing, and
  • human computation.
