Robert West

EPFL Data Science Lab

I’m an assistant professor in the School of Computer and Communication Sciences at EPFL, where I lead the Data Science Lab (dlab).

My research aims to make sense of large amounts of data by developing and applying algorithms and techniques in areas including

  • social and information network analysis,
  • machine learning,
  • computational social science,
  • data mining,
  • natural language processing, and
  • human computation.

For more research-related information, please check out the dlab website.

Some more info is available on my old Stanford webpage.
